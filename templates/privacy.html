Privacy

Privacy Policy for HBNI Audio Streaming Service

Effective Date: 2025-01-11

Last Updated: 2025-01-11

Welcome to HBNI Audio Streaming Service. This is an open-source project licensed under the MIT License. We respect your privacy and are committed to maintaining transparency about how this app operates and the data it collects.

1. Data We Collect

This app does not collect personal data from users. However, the following data is collected for functionality:

  • Playback Statistics: How often an archive is played and the last time it was accessed.
  • Broadcast Archives: All public broadcasts are archived and stored as part of the audio archive. Private broadcasts are not saved, recorded, or archived.
2. Push Notifications

This app sends push notifications for the following purposes:

  • Notifying users of scheduled events.
  • Notifying users of live events.
3. Scheduling and Broadcasting
  • Users can schedule events using a password for authentication.
  • Users with the correct password can broadcast audio through the app.
  • All public broadcasts will be archived and stored in the app's audio archive for future playback.
  • Private broadcasts are excluded from archiving and recording to ensure privacy.
4. Open Source Commitment

This app is open source and available under the MIT License. The code is publicly accessible, and users can verify its behavior and privacy practices.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure the limited data collected (playback statistics and broadcast archives) is securely stored. No personal data is collected, processed, or stored.

6. Your Rights

As this app does not collect personal data, there are no user-specific data rights beyond the ability to opt out of receiving push notifications via your device settings.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the app, you can reach us at jared@hbni.net.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as necessary. Any updates will be reflected here, and users are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
